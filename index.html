Hotsauce Crafting

Press C to start crafting. Select all the items you would like to combine.
Tap them again to deselect them. Press Done. The selected items have now been turned into a hotsauce.


If something goes wrong, reload the page.

But, beware, reloading will regenerate all enemies and chests.


Episode I – May the spice be with you.

A long time ago on farm far far away, the imperial forces of the chimichanga empire invaded planet agriculture. The evil imperial forces subdued most of the population and began enslaving all those that lived on planet agriculture. Within days the planet would be completely lost, if not for the rebellion. You, the leader of the rebellion, must make it through the isles of enemies and defeat the Emperor, master Pepper, named because he wears a pepper costume all the time... Yea, we know its weird. His weakness, and the weakness of all his minions is spiciness. Our suggestion, shoot hotsauce at them all. Go, do your mission!
